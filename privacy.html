Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and protected in line with ICO guidance, BACP ethical standards, BPS ethical frameworks, and UK law.

1. Data Collection & Use

Personal data such as name, contact details, and messages submitted via this website are collected solely for the purpose of responding to your enquiry and providing psychotherapy services. Data is not shared with third parties except where required by law.

2. Legal Basis & Ethical Compliance

  • Complies with UK Data Protection Act and ICO guidance.
  • Adheres to BACP ethical framework for good practice in psychotherapy.
  • Follows BPS Code of Ethics and Conduct where applicable.

3. Cookies & Website Tracking

This website may use necessary cookies to improve usability and session functionality. No third-party advertising cookies are used. Cookies can be disabled in your browser settings; essential cookies remain to ensure basic site functionality.

4. Data Retention

Contact form submissions and correspondence are stored only for as long as necessary to respond to your enquiry or maintain professional records in accordance with BACP requirements.

5. Data Security

Your information is protected through reasonable technical and organisational measures to prevent unauthorised access, disclosure, or loss. Only authorised personnel have access to personal data.

6. Your Rights

  • You may request access to the personal information we hold about you.
  • You may request correction or deletion of your data.
  • You may withdraw consent for contact communications at any time.

7. Contact for Privacy Matters

For questions or requests regarding your personal data, please contact Andy Myers at andymyerspsych@hotmail.com.

8. Updates to this Policy

This policy may be updated periodically to reflect legal and ethical requirements. Updates will be reflected on this page with a revised effective date.
